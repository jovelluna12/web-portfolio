## Overview
This project focused on maintaining and enhancing a **sports-themed job listing and social networking platform** originally developed around 2015.

The platform required modernization, bug fixes, and new feature development to restore usability and ensure long-term scalability.

## Challenges & Solutions

**Challenge:**  
Working with an outdated and inconsistently maintained codebase.

**Solution:**  
- Analyzed legacy architecture and identified high-risk areas  
- Applied incremental fixes and improvements instead of large rewrites  
- Ensured backward compatibility while introducing new features  

---

**Challenge:**  
Maintaining performance while adding new functionality.

**Solution:**  
- Optimized existing queries and frontend rendering  
- Reduced unnecessary processing in critical workflows  
- Tested updates thoroughly to avoid regressions  

---

## Results & Impact

- Helped revive a legacy platform for active use  
- Improved system stability and reduced recurring issues  
- Enabled the addition of new features without disrupting core functionality  

---

## What I Learned

- Legacy systems require a balance between caution and improvement  
- Small, consistent optimizations can significantly improve performance  
- Team collaboration is essential when working on long-term systems
